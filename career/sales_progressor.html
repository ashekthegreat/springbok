Sales Progressor

Location : Manchester
Realistic OTE £22-25k

Realistic OTE £22-25k, Basic Salary £16K-£18K p.a plus Commission. Plus guaranteed uncapped commission for 4 months to match your current earnings, while you build your own commission pipeline.

In-depth knowledge of the conveyancing process and exceptional organisational skills are essential.

Vacancy Description

We are looking for an organised senior Sales Progressor, to join our dynamic and growing business in Manchester 15 minutes' walk from Piccadilly. The successful candidate will manage their own personal portfolio of properties spread across the UK dealing with the property until successful exchange and completion.

What the role of Sales Progressor involves:

  • Looking after a manageable portfolio and exchanging a targeted number of properties which our current sales progressors are achieving comfortably
  • Liaising with vendor, purchasers, solicitors, financial advisors and other estate agents
  • Sending out memos, chasing access calls, dealing with surveys & renegotiations, helping clients fill out paperwork, speaking to solicitors, and anything that is required to progress the file to the point of exchange.
  • Up sell some of our recommended financial services / solicitors and get paid generously for these.
  • Update and maintain notes for your portfolio, and keep clients fully updated with the progress

Desirable profile:

  • Highly motivated
  • Exceptional organisation skills
  • Computer literate
  • Professional & excellent phone skills

Salary - Basic Salary of between £16k - £18K p.a. plus commission with a very realistic OTE £25k. plus guaranteed uncapped commission for 4 months to match your current earnings, while you build your own commission pipeline.
