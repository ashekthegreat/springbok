Regional Valuer

Location : All over the UK
Realistic OTE £45k

Minimum 3 years of property valuation experience required.

Achievable 1st year OTE of £40k+ and 2nd year OTE of £45k+
As a Property Valuer / marketing advisor you will attend property site visits and match the clients motivation and needs to one of our fast sale solutions. You will correctly identify the true value of properties and what price they need to be marketed at to generate sales within one month. Our most successful regional valuers are currently instructing in excess of 50 properties a month.

Responsibilities will include:

  • Attending 20+ property site visits per week
  • Ensuring a high conversion rate from site visit to instruction
  • Cross selling of financial services and preferred conveyancing companies
  • Intelligently pricing the property to ensure a 'fast sale'
  • After each site visit liaise with head office
  • Working to strict deadlines whilst maintaining the standards expected by the company
  • Working to targets

Salary - Basic Salary £22K p.a. with a very realistic OTE £40k in first year and £45k+ in year two. Plus guaranteed uncapped commission for 4 months to match your current earnings, while you build your own commission pipeline.

What's in it for you? Uncapped bonus- limited only by YOU
