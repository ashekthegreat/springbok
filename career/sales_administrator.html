Sales Administrator

Location : Manchester
Realistic OTE £14,000 - £16,000

Basic salary £14,000 - £16,000 depending on experience plus target bonus (realistic earnings £16,000-£17,000 OTE).

Looking for an exciting new position with a successful, innovative company that believes in rewarding its employees - Look no further!

Springbok Properties is looking for a full-time, experienced administrator/lister

What will you be doing?

  • Write up property details and upload images on to the system
  • Listing new properties from your portfolio of properties
  • Call clients from time to time for updates on listing
  • All basic aspects of office administration

Who are we looking for?

  • Excellent IT Skills (Microsoft Word, Excel and Email)
  • At least one yearadministration work experience
  • Well organised and methodical approach
  • Polite, friendly and professional telephone manner
  • Excellent communication skills, enthusiasm to learn and develop new skills.

Who will you be working for?

Springbok Properties is a fast expanding company moving to cover the whole of the England and Scotland. We are far exceeding traditional estate agents by achieving fast sales for our clients.

We provide an exciting, fast-paced environment for our employees with opportunities for progression as we believe in promoting top performers from within based on merit, success and hard work

A competitive basic salary and great commission scheme in place. This is an opportunity to join a fantastic company. DO NOT MISS OUT, Apply Today!!

If you have a background in sales administration, admin, lister, listing, office administration, we would be interested in hearing from you.
