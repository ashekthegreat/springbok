Property Sales Consultant

Location : Manchester
Realistic OTE £30,000 - £38,000

Basic plus Uncapped commission (realistic earnings £30,000-£38,000 OTE). Plus guaranteed uncapped commission for 4 months to match your current earnings, while you build your own commission pipeline.

Looking for an exciting new position with a successful, innovative company that believes in rewarding its employees - Look no further!

Our client requires full time experienced inbound/outbound telesales professionals.

This is not your average telesales role or average company - We are not shy about rewarding hard work. You must be a sales-driven, career focused and dedicated individual. If you get motivated by money, this is the job for you.

What's in it for you?

  • Basic Salary: £18,000
  • Commission: uncapped with realistic OTE of £30,000 to £38,000
  • Guaranteed uncapped commission for 4 months to match your current earnings, while you build your own commission pipeline.
  • Opportunities to progress to team leader and managerial positions for top performers
  • Opportunity to progress in other sections of the business.
  • No cold calling and no drumming up your own business
  • ALL inbound and outbound leads are provided and are warm

What will you be doing?

  • Qualifying inbound/outbound enquiries from clients
  • Converting the enquiries into appointments for our property valuers.
  • Organisation and time management of property valuers' diaries
  • Effective management of non-contacted leads and follow ups
  • Effective management of unsuccessful appointments.
  • Working to daily individual and team targets

Who will you be working for?

Our client, unlike traditional estate agencies that are performing poorly is a fast expanding company moving to cover the whole of the England and Scotland. We are far exceeding traditional estate agents by achieving fast sales for our clients.

We provide an exciting, fast-paced environment for our employees with opportunities for progression as we believe in promoting top performers from within based on merit, success and hard work

Who are we looking for?

No previous property experience is required, but the right attitude is essential.

You need to be:

  • driven and money hungry with experience in either inbound or outbound telesales...
  • career focused and possess a strong work ethic and customer focus.
  • able to probe clients to find out how best to help them
  • customer service focused with a demonstrable track record of success in B2C telesales
  • self-motivated, enthusiastic with bags of personality and a can do attitude

Salary OTE is uncapped with an average take home of £30+There is a competitive basic salary and great bonus scheme in place.

Hours

40 hours per week Monday to Friday, plus every other Saturday 9am - 3pm
